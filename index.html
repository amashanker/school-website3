Westmead Public School

Westmead PS has an outstanding academic reputation in the community as a School of Excellence. Learning opportunities are provided for students within a stimulating and secure environment. The school provides a planned and comprehensive approach to continuously improving student learning outcomes.

Students take responsibility for their learning. They are encouraged to progress in their learning by taking risks within supportive student-teacher relationships. Relationships between home and school are actively supported and developed so that parents know what children have learnt and what they will be learning next.

School Life

school entrance

it was established in 1917

When you just enter from the main entrence, to your right, you will see the years 3-6 library and above it are some of the year 3 classes. On the left, you have the main office. That is where students go if they get sevierely injured or if you want to enroll into the school. Parents can also pick you up if you have leave for the day in the middle of class.



school library

it was established in 1998

Our year 3-6 library was used to be a store room for books for the old library. Now, the librarians and books have moved intto a small, conjusted library for years 3-6.WPS Library has opened its doors and ready for a fun filled year ahead with Mrs Azar (Mon - Fri) and Mrs Kougellis (Mon - Thurs) working with our students in the library this year.



lecture theatre

it was established in 2000

My school used to be a University too. So, the built a lecture theatre for university students. Now, it is used by all the students and teachers in the school for meetings, events and extra curricular activities. We have a program called RFF, which is where RFF teachers teach student dance, drama, photography, road safety and water saftey. They sometimes teach in the lecture theatre when it is wet weather.
